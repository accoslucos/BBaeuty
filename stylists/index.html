Get To Know Your Austin Hairdressers

Experience You Can Depend On

When it comes to your beauty, you’d be hard pressed to find a better hairdresser. Silk Hair Salon boasts almost 70 years’ worth of combined experience in delivering the cuts, colors, and styles that make people love what they see in the mirror. Our hairdressers come from diverse backgrounds, and each brings a unique skill set to our Austin beauty parlor. We are professionals who love what we do, and we’d love the chance to work with you!

Classic & Contemporary Style

At our hair salon, we serve clients with a wide range of beauty needs. Some are searching for a completely new look while others depend on us to maintain and refine their style to perfection. We have the skills and resources to help every client that comes through our doors. We are happy to provide styling advice and suggestions, but we can also follow your specific instructions. Read below for more about the specific talents of our hairdressers.

LILI TYPHAIR

Master Stylist / Colorist / Manager

Lili is a Master Stylist with 11 years experience and is the Manager for Silk Hair Salon. The foundation of her work is her attention to detail, customer service and creative ability. She is a diverse stylist and brings her artistic energy to anything from classic and chic to trendy and funky. Lili is consistently striving to exceed customers’ expectations. Her love for artistry shows with every smile that leaves her chair.

Lili is also a DevaCurl Certified Stylist specializing in curly hair and is also certified in hair extensions.

Veronica GONZALES 

@hairwhatsup

Hair Stylist / Colorist

After exploring many career options, Veronica finally decided that her hobby should be put to work. She found an instant connection with the Paul Mitchell education. The culture they encourage caters to the client's goals and limitations. This union of creativity and customer service was a perfect fit for Veronica. She graduated from the Cosmetology Career Center through the Phase II Honors program in October 2005. Veronica has spent her entire cosmetology career working in her hometown of Austin, Texas.  She specializes in red hair colors, round brush techniques and Pure Form hair cutting. October 2012 brought her the Austin Monthly magazine's readers choice win for Best Stylist for Curly Hair.

"I feel like it's important to be able to provide a client's cut, color and style experience, completely. The best part about my job is transforming my client's confidence and attitude with one visit." - Veronica

JENNIFER COBURN

Hair Stylist / Colorist

Jennifer became a stylist 26 years ago when she moved to Austin to attend Baldwin Beauty Schools. Her professional career began when she earned her Instructor License and started teaching her students the art of “Cut And Color” for 10 years. Furthering her career, she then worked as a stylist in South Austin for 14 years before joining the team at Silk Hair Salon. Jennifer loves doing highlights, color, and the latest cuts and styles, as well as formal hair styling for special events. She also specializes in the Coppola Keratin Smoothing Complex. Her devoted clientele and constant referrals are evidence of Jennifer’s respect for both her client’s needs and the growth of her career. When Jennifer is not busy catering to her clientele, she and her husband are spending time with their 3 rescued dogs and cat.

LESLIE LEWIS

Hair Stylist / Colorist

Leslie has been styling the hair of her clients in Austin for over 20 years. Her expertise stems from her inherent understanding of her client’s wishes, paired with the properties of their hair, to achieve the desired result. Well skilled in color correction, she can make the worst color issue look great again, with minimal stress to her client’s hair. Her friendly personality makes her clients feel as if they are having their hair done by a close friend, not just a stylist. When she’s not making hair in Austin attractive, Leslie divides her time between her love of cats and fast cars. She works with local rescue organizations to find feral and abandoned cats new “forever” homes. Her love of fast cars includes Corvettes, Firebirds and Vipers-many of which she’s owned. Whether it’s a leisurely cruise or an all-out power run, she handles these cars like she handles her blow dryer: with precision and excellent results!

SUSAN ABBOTT

Hair Stylist / Colorist

Susan Abbott has a degree in fine art and science and views each client as an art project in the making. She was the previous owner of SESSIONS Salon in Costa Mesa, CA, and Studio Blue in Dayton, OH. Susan brings to Austin an artistic, intellectual, modern approach to hairstyling. Consultations are free! Come in and let her disclose all the fun options hiding in your hair.
